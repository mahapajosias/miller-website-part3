About Our School

Miller High School (Seshego, Polokwane) is dedicated to academic excellence, character development and inclusive education. We offer a variety of extra-mural activities to foster talent and teamwork.

miller is a Public high School located in polokwane seshego. The school falls within polokwane District Municipality in Limpopo. On this page, you will find all useful information about high School, subjects, registration form, extra-mural activities, and more. miller has a number of extra-mural activities available to students. Participating in activities that learners are passionate about,can increase brain function, and help learners to concentrate and manage their time better.
